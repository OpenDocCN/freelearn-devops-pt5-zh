Section 1: Preparing for Jira Align

In this section, you will be introduced to Jira Align and will also learn what kinds of organizations can benefit from implementing Jira Align. This section addresses the key success factors for an organization implementing Jira Align: people, processes, and tools. With Jira Align, we will check how to create and refine a backlog, and collaborate with everyone in the team.
